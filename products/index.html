    Why OrbitBits

    Built for the operator who needs to trust every step of the process.

    OrbitBits products share a common design principle: explicit behavior over convenience, native performance over abstraction, and full visibility into what the tool is actually doing on your system.

    One problem, done right

    Each tool owns a specific problem and solves it completely — no sprawling feature sets, no hidden behavior, no guesswork about what runs under the hood.

    Native performance by default

    Every binary is Rust-native. That means fast startup, low memory overhead, and binaries that behave the same way regardless of the environment they run in.

    Scriptable and composable

    OrbitBits CLIs follow Unix conventions — predictable output, exit codes that mean something, and interfaces that fit naturally into pipelines and automation.
