    Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

    Last updated: March 5, 2026

    This Acceptable Use Policy defines the rules for using OrbitBits services, software, and online resources.

    Purpose

    This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) defines acceptable and prohibited uses of OrbitBits services, software, and online resources.

    Prohibited Activities

    You may not use OrbitBits resources to:

    • Violate any applicable laws or regulations
    • Distribute malware or malicious software
    • Perform unauthorized access to systems or networks
    • Harass, abuse, or harm other individuals
    • Infringe intellectual property rights

    Abuse and Misuse

    Activities that attempt to exploit or misuse OrbitBits infrastructure, services, or open-source projects may result in restriction or removal of access.

    Reporting Abuse

    If you encounter abuse involving OrbitBits resources, please report it to:

    contact@orbitbits.com
